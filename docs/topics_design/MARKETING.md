## What It Does

Experient reads every open-ended response, NPS comment, support ticket, and free-text survey answer — and automatically surfaces the themes that matter, ranked by the signals your team actually cares about: urgency, sentiment trajectory, customer effort, NPS impact, and emotion.

Not word clouds. Not keyword counts. Actual structured intelligence.

**"Why are our promoters churning?"** — Experient finds the topic cluster with growing volume, stable NPS, but a quiet effort spike that your team would have missed for another quarter.

**"What should we fix first?"** — Every topic carries a composite urgency score built from sentiment severity, response volume, trend direction, and customer effort signals. The most urgent problem rises to the top automatically.

**"Is this getting better or worse?"** — Every topic is tracked week over week. Emerging, growing, worsening, fading, stable — Experient classifies the trajectory so you spend your time acting, not calculating.

## The Signal, Not the Noise

### Health Labels Tell You What the Trend Means

Every topic in Experient carries a health label that answers the question you actually have:

- **Emerging** — A new theme is forming. Worth watching.
- **Growing** — Volume up >25% week-over-week. Customers are increasingly talking about this.
- **Worsening** — Volume stable but sentiment dropping. Something that was fine is deteriorating.
- **Fading** — Volume down >30%. This topic is resolving, or customers have stopped mentioning it.
- **Stable** — No significant change. Keep an eye on it, but no action needed today.

### XM Signal Fingerprint

Every topic carries a full experience signal fingerprint:

- **NPS impact** — How much does this topic move your promoter-to-detractor ratio?
- **CSAT impact** — How much does this topic drag or lift satisfaction scores?
- **Driver score** — Point-biserial correlation: is mentioning this topic predictive of a low overall score?
- **Emotion distribution** — Across Plutchik's 8 primary emotions, what are customers feeling when they talk about this topic?
- **Top verbatims** — The actual customer words that best represent the topic's sentiment, selected automatically.
- **Urgency score** — A composite of sentiment severity, volume, effort, and trend direction. The number that tells you what to fix first.

## Built for Teams That Move Fast

### Insights in the time it takes to read them

Experient's pipeline runs asynchronously. Trigger a re-analysis after uploading a new batch of responses, and the dashboard updates in the background. No waiting for an analyst. No scheduling a report.

### Works for any survey type

NPS surveys, CSAT surveys, open-ended research, employee engagement — Experient adapts to the data. Score-only surveys get synthetic text synthesis. Open-text surveys get full semantic analysis. Mixed surveys use the richest signal available.

### Scales with your volume

Under the hood, Experient uses incremental vector clustering: new responses are matched to existing topic centroids in a single ANN lookup. You don't re-process everything from scratch on every run. A survey with 10,000 responses runs as fast as one with 100.
