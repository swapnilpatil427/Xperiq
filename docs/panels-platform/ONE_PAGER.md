# Experient Human Qualitative Panels
## Executive One-Pager

> **Date:** 2026-06-26

## The Problem

Every experience management platform tells you **WHAT** is happening — NPS dropped 7 points,
CSAT is 3.6, 42% of employees feel disengaged. None of them tell you **WHY** with the
speed and economics that modern teams need.

Traditional qualitative research costs $8,000–$25,000 per study and takes 6–10 weeks.
So teams skip it. They make multi-million dollar decisions from bar charts and averages,
with no human story behind them.

The tools that do exist — UserTesting, dscout, Dovetail — are isolated. Your NPS data
lives in one place. Your qual findings live in a PowerPoint deck that gets emailed once
and forgotten.

---

## The Solution

**Experient Human Qualitative Panels** is the first qualitative research platform built
natively inside an experience management system.

Researchers recruit directly from their existing survey respondents — no cold outreach,
no panel providers. They run async text communities, video interviews, and diary studies
in minutes, not weeks. AI handles transcription and thematic analysis. Crystal connects
qual findings back to the quant metrics that triggered the investigation.

The result: a qual study launched in an afternoon, insights delivered in hours, and
themes that link back to your NPS scores, CSAT data, and employee engagement trends.

---

## Why Now

Three forces converge:

1. **LLMs crossed a quality threshold** for qualitative analysis. Thematic coding that
   took a researcher 15 hours now takes 3 minutes with human review. The accuracy is
   defensible to enterprise research buyers.

2. **The XM market is moving upstream.** Qualtrics charges $150k+/year for what customers
   actually need. Mid-market companies (500–5,000 employees) have research needs but not
   research budgets. Self-serve, credit-based qual fills that gap.

3. **Research teams are leaner than ever.** A single researcher managing a qual program
   needs AI to handle the mechanics. The insight is the job. The transcription, coding,
   and scheduling are not.

## The Product — Five Things That Matter

**1. Recruit from people who already answered your surveys.**
Filter respondents by NPS score, CSAT, survey date, plan tier, or any field — and
invite them to a qual study in two clicks. No external panel. No 3-week recruiting cycle.

**2. Async-first. No scheduling required.**
Send a 5-day activity sequence to 20 participants. They respond in their own time,
on their phone, in their language. You get breadth without the logistics.

**3. AI thematic coding that a researcher can trust.**
Crystal proposes themes with supporting quotes. The researcher reviews, merges, and
approves. The AI does the first pass; the human applies judgment. No black box.

**4. Video sessions with automatic transcription.**
Schedule 1:1 interviews directly in the platform. Sessions are recorded, transcribed
with speaker labels, and analyzed. Clip any quote to a shareable video moment in seconds.

**5. The qual-quant bridge — the thing no one else has.**
Crystal detects an NPS drop → proposes a qual investigation → recruits from your
detractors → analyzes their responses → surfaces the "why" → tags it back to your
metric. One closed loop, one platform.
