# Xperiq Brand Guide

**Version 1.0 — June 2026**
**Status: Active**

## 1. Who We Are

### The Company
Xperiq is an AI-powered experience management platform that helps organisations create surveys, collect responses, and generate deep insights — automatically. We believe every business, from a five-person startup to a global enterprise, deserves world-class experience intelligence. That is what "for everyone" means.

### The Brand Description
**Experience + IQ**

This is the clearest way to describe what Xperiq does. We take experience data (surveys, feedback, responses) and add intelligence (AI analysis, Crystal copilot, dimensional insights) to produce answers that actually move a business forward.

Use "Experience + IQ" in:
- Investor conversations
- Sales introductions
- About pages and bios
- Conference booth descriptions

## 2. Brand Name

### Name
**Xperiq**

### Pronunciation
**"ex-PEER-ik"** — rhymes with "empiric"

Break it down: X-per-iq. The "X" is always capitalised. The "q" is always lowercase.

### Where the name comes from
- **X** = Experience (also signals "next-generation")
- **per** = through, by means of
- **iq** = Intelligence Quotient — the measure of intelligence

Together: intelligence through experience. Or simply: Experience + IQ.

### Name rules
| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Xperiq | XPERIQ |
| Xperiq | xperiq |
| Xperiq Inc. (legal) | Xperiq, Inc. (no comma unless legally required) |

### Spelling variants to avoid
- Xperique — too French
- Xperiq™ — only in trademark filings
- eXperiq — not our style

## 3. AI Sub-Brand: Crystal by Xperiq

Crystal is Xperiq's AI copilot. It is deeply embedded in the product and gets its own sub-brand treatment.

### The relationship
- **Xperiq** = the platform (surveys, data, insights, workflows)
- **Crystal** = the AI that powers intelligence within Xperiq

### How to refer to Crystal
| Context | Phrasing |
|---------|----------|
| First mention in marketing | Crystal by Xperiq |
| Repeated mentions | Crystal |
| Product UI | Crystal |
| Press release | Crystal, Xperiq's AI copilot |
| Tagline pairing | Crystal — Xperiq Copilot |

### Why Crystal works as a name
- Crystals are clear, precise, and multi-faceted — like good data analysis
- Crystal = clarity (the opposite of murky survey data)
- The hexagonal crystal shape is already the visual identity of the app's AI interface
- Crystal sounds friendly and approachable, not robotic

### Crystal is NOT
- A chatbot
- A search engine
- A data dashboard
Crystal is a **copilot** — it works alongside you, explains what the data means, and proposes actions.

## 4. Tagline

### Primary tagline
**"Intelligence for Every Experience"**

This tagline does three things:
1. States what we deliver (Intelligence)
2. States the scope (Every — not just enterprise, not just big companies)
3. Anchors the product category (Experience)

### Secondary tagline
**"Experience, understood."**

Use this for:
- Email sign-offs in marketing campaigns
- Short-form social posts
- Pull quotes in design

### What not to use as a tagline
- Anything with "data-driven" — overused
- "Actionable insights" — every analytics tool says this
- "The future of XM" — vague and unmeasurable

## 5. Colors

The existing design system colors are excellent and should be kept. They are modern, distinctive, and already deeply embedded in the product code.

### Primary palette

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Xperiq Blue | `#2a4bd9` | Primary buttons, links, active states, logo |
| Xperiq Blue Dim | `#173dcd` | Hover states on primary |
| Blue Container | `#879aff` | Tinted backgrounds, chips, badges |

### Accent palette

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Crystal Purple | `#8329c8` | Crystal AI elements, premium features, accent CTAs |
| Purple Container | `#d299ff` | Crystal tinted backgrounds |

### Secondary palette

| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Teal | `#00647c` | Secondary actions, data elements |
| Teal Container | `#82deff` | Teal backgrounds |

### Neutral palette
White `#ffffff` for backgrounds, near-black `#0f172a` for primary text.

### Color rules
- **Xperiq Blue is the brand anchor.** Use it for anything that needs to say "this is Xperiq."
- **Crystal Purple is for AI.** Any feature powered by Crystal gets purple treatment.
- **Teal is supporting.** Use for data, charts, and secondary UI — never as the primary brand color.
- **Never** use both blue and purple at the same visual weight on one element. One leads, one supports.

## 6. Typography

### Heading font: Manrope
Use for: all headings (H1–H4), logo wordmark, hero text, slide titles.
- Weight for logo: Bold (700)
- Weight for H1: ExtraBold (800)
- Weight for H2–H3: SemiBold (600)

### Body font: Inter
Use for: body copy, UI labels, captions, emails, forms.
- Weight for body: Regular (400)
- Weight for labels/captions: Medium (500)

### Where to get these fonts
Both are free on Google Fonts (fonts.google.com). They are already loaded in the Xperiq app.

### Typography rules
- **Never mix Manrope with a serif font.** Xperiq is clean and modern — no Times New Roman.
- Minimum body text size: 14px digital / 10pt print
- Line height for body copy: 1.6
- Line height for headings: 1.2

## 7. Logo

### Logo concept (to commission)
The Xperiq logo has two elements:

**1. The Crystal Mark**
A hexagonal facet shape — six sides, with interior lines suggesting the facets of a crystal. This is already the visual identity of the app's Crystal AI orb. It should be rendered in Xperiq Blue (`#2a4bd9`) with a subtle Crystal Purple (`#8329c8`) accent on one facet to suggest the AI layer.

**2. The Wordmark**
"Xperiq" set in Manrope Bold. The "X" at the start can optionally be given the same Crystal mark treatment — but test both with and without to see which reads cleaner at small sizes.

### Logo variants needed
| Variant | Use |
|---------|-----|
| Full logo (mark + wordmark, horizontal) | Website header, marketing materials |
| Full logo (mark + wordmark, stacked) | Square formats, app loading screen |
| Icon only (crystal mark) | Favicon, app icon, social profile images |
| Wordmark only | Email signatures, co-branding |
| White version (on dark) | Dark backgrounds, presentations |
| Black/mono version | Print, documents |

### Logo clear space
Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "X" in the wordmark. Nothing (text, images, other logos) should enter this zone.

### Logo minimum sizes
- Digital icon-only: 16×16px minimum
- Digital full logo: 120px wide minimum
- Print full logo: 1 inch wide minimum

## 8. Brand Voice

### Personality
Xperiq speaks like a smart, experienced colleague — not a salesperson, not a robot.

| We are | We are not |
|--------|-----------|
| Confident | Arrogant |
| Clear | Dumbed-down |
| Human | Robotic |
| Informed | Jargon-heavy |
| Encouraging | Hype-y |

### Voice in practice

**Too corporate:**
> "Xperiq leverages cutting-edge AI capabilities to deliver actionable insights across the enterprise experience management landscape."

**Too casual:**
> "Xperiq is like, super smart AI that helps you understand your surveys lol"

**Just right:**
> "Xperiq turns your survey data into answers you can act on — automatically, in seconds."

### Key phrases to use
- "Experience intelligence" (not "experience analytics")
- "Crystal answers" (not "AI responses")
- "Understand your customers" (not "leverage customer data")
- "For everyone" (not "enterprise-grade" when speaking to smaller customers)

### Key phrases to avoid
- "Synergy"
- "Best-in-breed"
- "Game-changing"
- "Disruptive"
- "Robust"
- "Seamless" (overused by every SaaS company)

## 9. Brand in Context

> "Xperiq is an AI-powered experience management platform. You run surveys and collect feedback — Xperiq's AI, called Crystal, automatically turns that data into insights and tells you what to do about them. Think of Qualtrics, but with an AI copilot built in from the ground up, and priced for teams of all sizes."

### One-liner for social profiles
> "Experience + IQ. Survey intelligence powered by Crystal AI."

### Company description (100 words — for press kits, About pages)
> Xperiq is an AI-powered experience management platform that helps organisations understand and act on their customer, employee, and market data. Built around Crystal — an AI copilot trained on experience science — Xperiq automatically transforms survey responses and feedback into dimensional insights: descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive. Unlike traditional XM platforms built for enterprise IT teams, Xperiq is designed for every team: HR, CX, operations, marketing, and product. From a five-person startup to a global brand, Xperiq delivers intelligence for every experience.

## 10. What This Brand Is NOT

To keep the brand sharp, be clear about what Xperiq is not positioning itself as:

| Not this | Why |
|----------|-----|
| A survey tool | We are an intelligence platform — surveys are just the input |
| A dashboard company | Dashboards show data; Xperiq explains it |
| An enterprise-only product | "For everyone" is a core differentiator |
| A Qualtrics clone | We are AI-native; they bolted AI on |
| A chatbot product | Crystal is a copilot, not a support bot |
