The model · flat plan + bundled AI allowance

Four tiers. One transparent meter.

FREE

$0

3 surveys · 100 responses/mo · 3 insight runs + 5 Crystal turns (lifetime). The funnel.

STARTER

$49/mo

2,000 responses · 1,500 AI credits · 3 seats. Surveys + reporting + light AI.

GROWTH ★

$299/mo

20K responses · 12K AI credits · 10 seats. Crystal, Cases, Broadcasts. The profit engine.

ENTERPRISE

$1,499/mo+

Unlimited responses · 80K credits · XO-Fusion · dept pools · RBAC · SLA.

1 credit = $0.01. AI credits are consumed only by insight runs (50), Crystal turns (15), XO-Fusion (200). Core usage is bundled — no meter.
